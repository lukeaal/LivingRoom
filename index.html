CashMap - IOS app for location based spending awareness

Cashmap lets you log your purchases, along with where you placed them in real time. A visual map of your location is used trends of where you make the most purchases. After enough purchases have been logged in a certain area the CashMap will notify the user if they have reached within 5 meters of where they have spent a considerable amount before. All in an effort to make the use aware of their spending habits.
